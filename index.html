About Chai Shaukeen Cafe

Chai Shaukeeen - “A sip before everything”.

Chai Shaukeen is the new startup of Jalandhar based engineer who loves tea very much and then came up with the idea of setting up tea cafe to sell tea and make people happy . Our Chai is made with fresh, natural, and high quality ingredients. we deliver the highest quality tea and snacks to give you the best experience. That's How every sip of chai at Chai Shaukeen is made special.

We wanted to serve best quality tea and snacks at affordable rates and cater to the out of home tea craving for students, office goers, senior citizens and families. Our aim is to facilitate the availability of organic tea, food along with a nice and inexpensive place to hang out. We wanted to become a part of everyone's day-to-day life and we did.

We are eagerly ready to welcome our new customers in our chai Shaukeen family. So come and become part of our family.

Thank you.

Pankaj Sasmal (Founder)
